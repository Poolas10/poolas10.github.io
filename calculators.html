Formula

FIB-4 = (Age × AST) ÷ (Platelets × √ALT)

Interpretation cutoffs (general population / NAFLD):
< 1.30 — Low risk of advanced fibrosis (F0–F1)
1.30 – 2.67 — Indeterminate, further evaluation recommended
> 2.67 — High risk of advanced fibrosis (F3–F4)

Reference: Sterling et al. Hepatology 2006 | AASLD Guidelines
